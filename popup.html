Routing Rules

▼

When enabled, queries are sent to your selected LLM if they:

  • End with a question mark (?)
  • Start with question words (what, when, where, etc.)
  • Contain explanation-seeking phrases (explain, help me understand)
  • Indicate problem-solving needs (troubleshoot, help)
  • Seek opinions or recommendations (recommend, pros and cons)
